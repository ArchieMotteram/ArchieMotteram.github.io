AI & the Australian Economy

A forecast of how artificial intelligence will reshape productivity, work, and inequality in Australia by 2030.

Thesis

By 2030, AI is likely to lift Australian productivity mainly through task automation and augmentation. However, without strong diffusion and policy, gains will be uneven, benefiting high-skill workers and large firms more than others.

Economic Channels

Productivity

AI reduces time spent on routine cognitive work.

Augmentation

Workers become more productive rather than replaced.

Innovation

Lower cost of experimentation and prototyping.

Concentration
