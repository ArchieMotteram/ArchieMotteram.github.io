AI as a General-Purpose Technology

AI fits the definition of a general-purpose technology (GPT) because it is widely applicable across industries, improves over time, and requires complementary organisational change.

Historical GPTs like electricity and computing only delivered major productivity gains when firms restructured workflows around them. The same is expected for AI in Australia.

In a service-based economy, AI is particularly powerful because it reduces the cost of information processing tasks across law, finance, healthcare, and education.
